## Key Learnings

1. Hospital admissions require coordination among providers, nurses, case managers, utilization review teams, and insurance companies.

2. Prior Authorization significantly affects admission readiness.

3. Documentation quality directly impacts approval outcomes.

4. Observation and Inpatient admissions have different billing and Medicare requirements.

5. Utilization Review teams use InterQual and Milliman criteria to evaluate medical necessity.

6. Administrative readiness and clinical readiness must both be addressed before admission.
