Business Strategy Report – Bhashyam
Executive Summary

Bhashyam is an AI-powered multilingual translation platform designed for Indian users. The platform enables translation of text, images, speech, and video subtitles across India's 22 official languages.

The growing need for regional language accessibility creates a strong opportunity for scalable adoption among students, educators, businesses, and content creators.

Key Opportunity

India's internet growth is increasingly driven by regional language users.

Key Risk

Users may rely on free alternatives such as Google Translate.
