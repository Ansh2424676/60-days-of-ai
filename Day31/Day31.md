# Day 31 – AI Supply Chain Control Tower

## Project Overview

Built an interactive AI Supply Chain Control Tower simulation using HTML, CSS, and Vanilla JavaScript.

The application simulates the role of a Head of Operations managing real-time supply chain disruptions including supplier delays, warehouse stockouts, transportation issues, customs inspections, and demand spikes.

---

## Features

- Real-time KPI Dashboard
- Dynamic Alert Generation
- Operational Decision Making
- Event Logging System
- Performance Scoring
- Difficulty Scaling
- Pause Functionality
- Help & Instructions Modal
- Responsive Design
- End-of-Game Performance Report

## Key Learnings

- Supply Chain Control Towers provide real-time operational visibility.
- Operational decisions directly affect business KPIs.
- Managing multiple disruptions requires prioritization.
- Revenue protection depends on proactive response strategies.
- Supply chain resilience improves through backup planning and rapid decision-making.
