## Key Learnings

1. Prior Authorization is a review process used by insurance companies before approving certain treatments.

2. Missing documentation is one of the biggest reasons for PA denials.

3. Denial does not mean permanent rejection.

4. Appeals can overturn denials when proper clinical evidence is provided.

5. PA delays can impact treatment timelines for serious conditions.

6. Healthcare systems monitor denial rates, appeal rates, and resolution times to improve operations.

## What I Learned

Building this simulator helped me understand how providers, insurers, and patients interact during the Prior Authorization process. I learned how documentation, eligibility checks, and appeals influence treatment approval.
