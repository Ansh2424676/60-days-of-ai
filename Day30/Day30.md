# Day 30 – Supply Chain Optimization with Claude

## Project

Supply Chain Builder

## Objective

Built an interactive Supply Chain Optimization Simulator using Claude AI to understand how sourcing, manufacturing, warehousing, transportation, and inventory decisions impact business performance.

## Key Learnings

### 1. Supply Chain Trade-offs

Every supply chain decision affects cost, speed, risk, customer satisfaction, and sustainability.

### 2. Supplier Diversification

Using multiple suppliers reduces dependency risk and improves business continuity.

### 3. Transportation Strategy

Different transportation modes create different balances between speed and cost.

### 4. Inventory Management

Balanced inventory strategies help avoid stockouts while minimizing storage costs.

### 5. Warehouse Optimization

Regional warehouses improve delivery speed and customer satisfaction but increase operating costs.

## Simulation Result

* Overall Supply Chain Score: 86/100
* Strongest Area: Customer Satisfaction
* Biggest Risk: Transportation Disruption
