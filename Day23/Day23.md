# Final Verdict

## 🟡 Promising but Unvalidated

### Why?

- Large market opportunity
- Real customer problem
- Strong education-focused niche
- Multiple monetization paths

### Challenges

- Competition is intense
- No paying customers yet
- Limited primary validation

### Recommendation

Proceed with a focused MVP targeting Students & Teachers before expanding into businesses, government offices, or creators.

---

# Key Learnings

- Build for one customer segment first.
- Validate demand before investing heavily in development.
- Focus on solving one problem exceptionally well.
- Use existing translation APIs before building custom AI models.
- Customer interviews are more valuable than assumptions.
