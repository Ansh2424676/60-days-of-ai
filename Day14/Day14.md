# Day 14 - AI Job Red Flag Detector

## Objective

Used Claude AI to evaluate a job opportunity and identify hiring risks, workplace concerns, and positive signals before applying.

## Overall Risk Score

42/100

## Top Red Flags

1. Missing salary information (7/10)
2. Fast-paced environment (5/10)
3. Vague company details (5/10)
4. Hybrid model without location details (4/10)
5. Generic and broad responsibilities (4/10)
6. No mentorship or training structure mentioned (3/10)

## Positive Signals

* Freshers can apply.
* Reasonable skill requirements (Python, SQL, Excel, Power BI).
* Responsibilities align with a Data Analyst Intern role.
* No "rockstar", "ninja", or hustle-culture language.
* Company size suggests an established operation.

## Risk Breakdown

| Category     | Risk Level  |
| ------------ | ----------- |
| Requirements | Low         |
| Culture      | Medium      |
| Remote       | Medium      |
| Hiring       | Medium-High |
| Company      | Medium      |

## Final Verdict

⚠️ Apply with Caution

The internship appears suitable for freshers, but salary transparency, company growth details, and hybrid work expectations should be clarified before proceeding.

## Smart Interview Questions

1. Could you share the stipend/salary range and whether this internship has a path to full-time conversion?
2. How many days a week is the hybrid model, and is the office location fixed or could it change?
3. What does "fast-paced" mean in daily work, and is overtime expected?
4. Who will be my mentor/manager, and what training is provided?
5. Can you share recent company growth metrics and whether there have been layoffs or restructuring?

## Key Learnings

* Job applications are a two-way evaluation process.
* Salary transparency is an important factor when assessing opportunities.
* Terms like "fast-paced environment" should be investigated further.
* Company information should be verified before investing time in interviews.
* AI can quickly identify risks and generate useful interview questions.
* Asking targeted questions helps avoid surprises later in the hiring process.
