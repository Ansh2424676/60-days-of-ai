## Key Insights

1. Fuel cost per kilometer varies significantly between fuel types.
2. E85 offers lower pump prices but may incur mileage penalties.
3. EV vehicles showed the lowest operational emissions.
4. Older vehicles tend to have higher maintenance costs.
5. Visualization helped identify cost and environmental trade-offs quickly.
---
## Learnings

* Claude can analyze CSV datasets effectively.
* AI can generate complete HTML dashboards from raw data.
* SVG charts provide lightweight and responsive visualizations.
* Business KPIs can be derived automatically from structured datasets.
* Data storytelling becomes easier through dashboards.
