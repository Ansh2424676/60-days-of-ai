# Day 29 – Operation Lifeline: Supply Chain Crisis Lab

## Overview

Today I built an enterprise supply chain crisis simulation using Claude AI.

The simulator generates a fictional company, introduces a random supply chain crisis, and challenges the player to make strategic decisions across operations, supplier negotiations, executive leadership, and AI investments.

## Features

* Random company generation
* Random crisis scenarios
* War Room response planning
* Supplier negotiation simulation
* CEO leadership assessment
* AI strategy investment decisions
* Executive dashboard with business metrics

## Key Learnings

1. Supply chain disruptions impact inventory, customer satisfaction, and profitability simultaneously.
2. Supplier relationships are critical during operational crises.
3. Leadership decisions require balancing short-term costs and long-term resilience.
4. AI tools can improve forecasting, procurement, and risk monitoring.
5. Effective crisis management depends on communication, planning, and rapid decision-making.

## Conclusion

This simulation helped me understand how enterprises manage supply chain disruptions and how AI can strengthen operational resilience.
